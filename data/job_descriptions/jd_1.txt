1. Software Tester / QA Engineer (Entry-Level)
Location: Bengaluru, India (Hybrid)
Experience: 0–2 years
Job Type: Full-time

Job Description:
We are looking for a passionate Software Tester to assess software quality through manual and automated testing. You will be responsible for finding and reporting bugs and glitches before product launches.

Responsibilities:

Review and analyze system specifications and requirements

Collaborate with developers to ensure quality throughout the SDLC

Execute test cases (manual and automated) and analyze results

Identify, document, and track bugs

Perform regression testing when bugs are resolved

Requirements:

Bachelor’s degree in Computer Science or related field

Understanding of software QA methodologies and tools

Familiarity with bug tracking tools like JIRA

Basic knowledge of Selenium or other automation frameworks

Attention to detail and excellent communication skills

Preferred:

ISTQB Certification

Experience testing web and mobile applications
